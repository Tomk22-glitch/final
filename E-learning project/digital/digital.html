1. Digital Safety

What is Cybersecurity? It’s the practice of protecting your devices and data from theft, misuse, or damage.

  • Why is it important? Hackers can steal passwords, money, or misuse your identity. Malware can damage your device.

Tips to Stay Safe

  • 🔑 Use strong passwords with symbols and numbers.
  • 🔄 Keep your software updated to fix security gaps.
  • 🔒 Lock your devices with a PIN or fingerprint.
  • ☁️ Back up important files to the cloud or a USB drive.
  • 👁️ Be careful about what you share online.

Recognizing Scams & Phishing

  • 😨 Be wary of urgent messages: “Your account will close in 24 hours!”
  • 🔗 Watch for suspicious links and weird senders.
  • ❌ Bad grammar and spelling errors are red flags.
  • Use antivirus software and only download trusted apps.

2. Digital Citizenship

What is a Digital Citizen? Someone who uses the internet safely, responsibly, and kindly.

What is a Digital Footprint? The trail of everything you do online — from posts to searches.

Good Online Behavior

  • ✅ Think before you post — your posts can last forever.
  • ✅ Be respectful. ❌ Don’t bully or embarrass others.
  • ✅ Use privacy settings. ❌ Don’t overshare personal details.

Respecting Intellectual Property

  • 📌 Give credit when you use someone’s image or idea.
  • 📚 Don’t copy-paste others’ work — write in your own words.
  • 🚫 Don’t download pirated software or movies — it’s illegal.
