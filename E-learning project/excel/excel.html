A spreadsheet is an electronic ledger for entering, editing, and manipulating data in a tabular format (rows & columns).

  • Manual Spreadsheet: Paper ledger books written by hand.
  • Electronic Spreadsheet: Software like Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.
